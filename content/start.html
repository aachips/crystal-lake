Mystery of Crystal Lake

Crystal Lake, a haven for families and nature lovers, faces a terrible crisis. The once vibrant lake, known for its crystal-clear water and abundant fish, has been declared unsafe due to mysterious contamination. The townspeople are in shock and desperate for answers. You, a young detective with a keen eye for detail, have been called upon to uncover the truth. Are you ready to begin your investigation?

Who will you talk to first?

Sheriff Jones

"The contamination seems deliberate," Sheriff Jones says with a grim expression. "Someone must have dumped something nasty in the lake. I've questioned a few folks, but no leads yet. Maybe you can find something I missed."

Dr. Evans

"The tests show high levels of industrial pollutants. It's definitely not natural. Long-term exposure could be harmful," Dr. Evans explains with concern. "Have you talked to the folks at the Pure Water Processing Plant? They might know something."

Mr. Miller

"This whole mess is a disaster for business!" Mr. Miller exclaims. "Fishin' licenses, bait sales, everything's down the drain. I heard some rumors about the factory dumping waste at night, but who knows what's true these days..."

A mix of posts appears: some express frustration, others share conspiracy theories, and a few offer unverified "evidence." One post mentions a strange light seen near the factory on the night of the contamination.
